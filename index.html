Middleburg Community Theatre

This Year's Production

Bumbling private eye Sam Club is back and better than ever. Don't miss this year's production of The Case of the Motorcoach Murders, starring North Florida's premier theatre troupe: the Mumcee Players!

We are a not for profit organization. Our goal is simple: to bring members of the community together for a night of food, fun, and laughter! We hold one weekend event every year with two showings of our production.

Behind The Scenes

"The truth is that this is more than just a play. This is a community event. We love our neighbors, we love our community, and we bring people together for dinner, laughter and fun - and that is the measure of our success

Arthur Dolder, Director
